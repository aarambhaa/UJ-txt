Our Story

Bringing India's Best Fabrics to Pokhara

Ujjwal Textile was founded with a simple mission — to bring the richness of Indian traditional clothing to the heart of Pokhara. We source directly from premier vendors across Rajasthan, Gujarat, Varanasi, and Lucknow.

Our carefully curated collections celebrate the artistry of Indian weavers and designers, offering our customers access to authentic fabrics and designs that tell a story of culture and craftsmanship. Whether you're dressing for a festival, a wedding, or an everyday occasion, we have something beautiful for you.
